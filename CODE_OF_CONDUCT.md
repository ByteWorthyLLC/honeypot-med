# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to be respectful, direct, and technically honest.

Examples of behavior that support a healthy project:

- debating ideas without personal attacks
- reporting gaps and regressions clearly
- sharing evidence when making security claims
- helping non-developers use the project safely

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or discrimination
- doxxing or privacy abuse
- intentionally misleading security claims
- publishing exploit material that creates unnecessary harm

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, close discussions, or block participation when behavior undermines contributor safety or the integrity of the project.
